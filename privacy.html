Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

This privacy policy applies to the "In My Shoes Podcast" application ("the app"), an internal automation tool used to publish podcast video clips to social media platforms (including TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook) on behalf of the In My Shoes podcast.

What this app does

The app uploads pre-recorded video clips, produced by the In My Shoes podcast, to connected social media accounts on a scheduled basis. It does not collect, process, or store any personal data from end users of TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook.

Data we access

The app uses official platform APIs (such as the TikTok Content Posting API) solely to authenticate the podcast's own social media accounts and to upload video content created by the podcast. The only data accessed is:

  • OAuth access/refresh tokens for the podcast's own connected accounts
  • Basic account identity information (e.g. username) needed to confirm the correct account is connected

Data we do not collect

This app does not collect data from viewers, followers, or any third party. It does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Data storage

OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only by this automation pipeline to publish content. They are never shared with third parties.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the podcast via the channels listed at In My Shoes Podcast.
